let us see the diffrent types of openings commonly used in chess games

  • ITALIAN GAME

    it is one if the oldest and powerful opening played at intermediate level or grandmaster level. There are multiple variation of this opening also you can quickly adjust your game accordingly where it is attack or defense.

  • RUY LOPEZ GAME

    it also called as spanish game, this opening is mostly played at grandmaster level like MAGNUS CARLSEN , GARRY KASPAROV etc.

    The main move order starts with

    1. e4 e5

    2. Nf3 Nc6

    3. Bb5

  • PONZIANI OPENING

    it is mostly played at begineers level or sometimes at intermediate level. here, main motive is to grab the postion of center of the board and then attack with queen later on. this opening is palyed by white pieces.

    Moves: 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.c3

  • STAFFORD GAMBIT

    it is played by black pieces, the Stafford Gambit is filled with deadly traps. A fun opening for the risk takers. here, main motive is to sacrifice a pawn to gain a quick development it is one of the deadliest attack if white does not play carefully.

    1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nf6 3.Nxe5 Nc6.

  • DANISH GAMBIT

    it is played by white pieces, this one is most aggressive opening, where white sacrifice his 2 pawns to gain very aggressive and commanding position , the main thing to keep in mind that white should not exchange pieces, the bishop pairs are imporatant and you have to be super aggressive while playing this opening.
